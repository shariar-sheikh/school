About the School

Our history, mission & identity

Founded in 1954, serving Kashiani for over 70 years with pride.

Our Story

Seven decades of service

Kashiani GS Pilot High School was established in 1954 by visionary community leaders who recognized the urgent need for quality secondary education in Gopalganj district.

Granted "Pilot" status by the Government of Bangladesh, the school was entrusted with demonstrating best practices that could be replicated across the region — shaping our identity as rigorous, inclusive, and community-rooted.

"This school did not merely educate us — it shaped who we became as citizens of Bangladesh."
— A 1978 alumnus, Senior Government Officer

Today, over 1,200 students across Classes 6–10 are served by a dedicated faculty of 48, across three academic streams.

Milestones

School timeline

1954

School Founded

Established with 80 students, Classes VI–VIII, by local community leaders of Kashiani thana.

1961

Government Recognition

Formally recognized as a Grade-A secondary institution by East Pakistan education authorities.

1975

Pilot Status Awarded

Designated as a Government Pilot High School by the Bangladesh Ministry of Education post-independence.

1990

Science Labs Built

Dedicated Physics, Chemistry, and Biology labs opened for practical curriculum delivery.

2005

Computer Lab Launched

30-seat ICT lab established, integrating digital education into the core curriculum.

2018

New Academic Building

4-storey academic block with modern classrooms, library wing, and staffroom complex completed.

2024

Record SSC Results

96% pass rate — 14 students achieved GPA-5.0, the best result in school history.

Academics

Curriculum, streams & facilities

Streams

Academic pathways

Science Stream

Physics · Chemistry · Biology · Higher Mathematics. Gateway to engineering, medicine, and research.

Humanities Stream

History · Geography · Civics · Economics. Pathways to law, social sciences, journalism, and public service.

Business Stream

Accounting · Business Studies · Finance & Banking. Foundations for commerce and management careers.

Class Structure

Year groups

Class VI – VIII · JSC Level

Core foundation: Bengali, English, Mathematics, General Science, Religion, Social Studies, ICT. Mixed-ability classrooms with a strong pastoral system.

Class IX – X · SSC Level

Stream specialization begins. Students choose Science, Humanities, or Business. Dedicated subject teachers, board exam prep, and tutorial support.

Facilities

Campus infrastructure

Science Labs

Separate labs for Physics, Chemistry, and Biology with apparatus for SSC practical examinations.

Computer Lab

30-seat lab with internet access. ICT is compulsory for all students from Class VI.

Library

4,000+ volumes — textbooks, references, literature, science journals, and newspapers.

Sports Ground

Full-size football and cricket ground. Annual sports day held every December.

Assembly Hall

500-capacity hall for morning assembly, cultural events, prize-giving, and parent meetings.

Prayer Hall

Dedicated space for Jumu'ah and daily prayers for students and staff.
